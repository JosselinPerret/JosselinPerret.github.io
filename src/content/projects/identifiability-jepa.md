## Project overview

This article explores whether JEPA objectives identify stable and meaningful latent factors, and under which assumptions identifiability can or cannot be guaranteed.

## Story and approach

I am currently structuring the article around three parts: formal setup and assumptions, known non-identifiability counterexamples, and practical constraints that may recover partial identifiability.
